Banana

High-value fruit crop, needs good water management.

Groundnut

Rich in oil, great for sandy soils.

Tomato

Needs moderate watering, thrives in warm weather.

Corn

High yield crop, prefers loamy soil.

Chilly

Grows well in hot climates, needs well-drained soil.
