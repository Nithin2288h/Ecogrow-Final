Papaya Growing Guide

1. Variety Selection

Choose good varieties like Red Lady, Coorg Honey Dew, or Pusa Delicious. Use healthy seedlings.

2. Climate & Soil

Warm climate, good sunlight, and well-drained sandy loam soils are ideal.

3. Planting

Space plants 2 m × 2 m apart. Water immediately after transplanting.

Follow these steps for high-quality papaya fruits.

For local help, visit your nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK).
