## 🚀 How to Use

### 1. Access the Website
- Go to: http://127.0.0.1:5000/
- Click "🛒 Crop Products & Orders"

### 2. Create Account
- Click "👤 Login / Signup"
- Click "Sign Up"
- Fill in details:
  - Full Name
  - Address
  - Account Type (Consumer or Farmer)
  - Email
  - Password (min 6 characters)
- Click "Sign Up"

### 3. Browse Products
- After login, go to "Crop Products"
- Browse by category
- Click "Order Now" on any product

### 4. Place Order
- Enter quantity
- Enter/confirm address
- Click "Place Order"
- Order appears in "My Orders"

### 5. Manage Orders (Farmer)
- Switch to Farmer account type
- Go to "Orders Management"
- View all orders
- Accept or Reject orders

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Firebase Authentication Setup:
1. Go to: https://console.firebase.google.com/

3. Enable Authentication:
   - Authentication → Sign-in method
   - Enable "Email/Password"
